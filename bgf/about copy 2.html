About Us

About us - Better Globe Forestry Ltd.

Since 2004, Better Globe Forestry has been a trusted partner in sustainable forestry and community development. With operations in Kenya and Uganda, we specialize in large-scale forestry and agroforestry projects, particularly in drylands, combining environmental restoration with lasting social and economic impact.

Our Commitment to Reforestation.

At Better Globe Forestry, we harness the power of trees to restore land, fight climate change, and support communities. With millions planted in Kenya and Uganda, our reforestation efforts focus on long-term impact through careful species selection, ongoing maintenance, and sustainable land management.

Social Responsibility and Community Development

Sustainability means empowering communities. We create jobs, support education, offer microfinance, and train in water management, improving lives where we plant trees.

Our Vision

To drive sustainable development in Africa by transforming drylands into thriving forests, fostering economic growth, environmental restoration, and community empowerment.

Greener Future

Better Globe Forestry empowers individuals and organizations to invest in sustainable forestry, delivering financial returns, social impact, and environmental benefits, the Triple Bottom Line. Join us in growing a greener future.

Growing a Greener Legacy

Partner with Better Globe Forestry to create lasting environmental, social, and economic value. Join us in reforesting drylands, empowering communities, and building a sustainable future for your business and the planet.
